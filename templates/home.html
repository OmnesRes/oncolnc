Welcome to OncoLnc

Here you can link TCGA survival data to lncRNA, mRNA, or miRNA expression levels. To get started simply input either a MiTranscriptome beta lncRNA, Tier 3 TCGA mRNA or miRNA. More information can be found at this publication, youtube, or Omnes Res. Site is in beta testing, if you get any server errors please email omnesresnetwork at gmail.com, and let me know what you did to get the error.
